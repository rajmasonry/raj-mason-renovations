About Raj Mason & Renovations

Raj Mason & Renovations is a professional masonry and renovation company serving residential and commercial clients. We take pride in delivering durable, high-quality workmanship on every project.


Our expertise includes bricklaying, stone masonry, precast concrete installation, block work, restoration, and complete renovation services. We are committed to reliability, safety, and customer satisfaction.
